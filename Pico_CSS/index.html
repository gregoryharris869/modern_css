About

I'm Gregory Harris, a frontend web developer based in Seattle with a passion for crafting beautiful, responsive user interfaces. I discovered my love for coding during college, where I majored in Computer Science, but it wasn’t until I started working on personal projects that I realized how much I enjoyed bringing designs to life in the browser. I specialize in React and Tailwind CSS, and I thrive on turning complex design concepts into clean, functional, and accessible web experiences. Currently, I work at a fast-paced tech startup, where I lead a small frontend team and help shape our UI architecture. Outside of work, I enjoy contributing to open-source projects and writing blog posts that share what I’ve learned with other developers.
